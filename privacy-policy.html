Privacy Policy

This privacy policy below is only applicable to Mafia Remastered. In this privacy policy, any time the word we or us is used, it is referred to Mafia Remastered.


Introduction

Mafia Remastered is a discord bot application designed to allow discord users to play Mafia on Discord! Players can join using the /join command, or click a button to join a game and play. When you play Mafia Remastered, the game logs your user id and info to give you awards for achievements or rewards.

Mafia Remastered is comitted in ensuring that your data is protected. Mafia Remastered adheres to laws related to data protection to ensure a smooth experience when you use Mafia Remastered. This Privacy Policy updated on February 8, 2024.


Information Collected

When you use our bot for the first time, you give consent for Mafia Remastered to store your data for various purposes. The data we collect is your Discord user ID that is used as a way to identify between different users. We also store all prompts you submit through Mafia Remastered, through modals and commands, that includes, but is not limited to, Last Will Customization, Custom Mafia Death Message and Last Wills.

For Last Wills and other game related data, they will be only stored until the game ends, which at most, lasts 5 hours. Game related data includes your action towards other users on Discord, your current status on the game, and the role that has been generated to you.

Mafia Remastered also stores game logs that includes the user's Discord username, for 5 days before deleting the logs to respect user privacy. After a new game has ended, the button to view the game logs will show the logs for the latest game instead. The game log feature can be turned off by Server Administrators at any time for their Discord Server.


Use of Data

Your Discord user ID is stored to distinguish you and other players when displaying your statistics. These statistics includes achievements, current balance (fake currency), win statistics and role statistics. Your statistics can be viewed by other people using Mafia Remastered.
Your info sent through modals and commands are used for game purposes. Your last will is shown when you die in the game, and your custom mafia death message is shown when you die to the mafia in the game. Your last will customization is used to display a more vibrant embed once you die, acting like a template.
Your Discord Username is stored in game logs, if the feature is enabled by Server Administrators. Any buttons and select menus you clicked during the game is also stored to ensure that you are not AFK, and to show in the game logs about your actions.


Data Retention and Deletion

As stated above, we collect your Discord user ID, your Discord username, the prompts you enter when using Mafia Remastered and the buttons or select menus you click. You can always request to remove your data at any given time by joining the support server. Everything related to your data will be wiped.
