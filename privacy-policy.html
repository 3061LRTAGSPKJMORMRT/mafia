Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy was updated on April 27, 2024.
This Privacy Policy will take effect on May 1, 2024.

This privacy policy below is only applicable to Mafia Remastered. In this privacy policy, any time the word we or us is used, it is referred to Mafia Remastered.

Any time we mention the word game, it is referred to a simulation of a game that is played on Discord, and not any third-party game.


Introduction

Mafia Remastered is a discord bot application designed to allow discord users to play Mafia on Discord! Players can join using the /join command, or click a button to join a game and play. When you play Mafia Remastered, the game logs your user id and info to give you awards for achievements or rewards.

Mafia Remastered is comitted in ensuring that your data is protected. Mafia Remastered adheres to laws related to data protection to ensure a smooth experience when you use Mafia Remastered.


Information Collected

When you use our bot for the first time, you give consent for Mafia Remastered to store your data for various purposes. The data we collect is your Discord user ID that is used as a way to identify between different users. We also store all prompts you submit through Mafia Remastered, through modals and commands, that includes, but is not limited to, Last Will Customization, Custom Mafia Death Message and Last Wills.

For Last Wills and other game related data, they will be only stored until the game ends, which at most, lasts 5 hours. Game related data includes your action towards other users on Discord, your current status on the game, and the role that has been generated to you.

Mafia Remastered also stores game logs that includes the user's Discord username, for 5 days before deleting the logs to respect user privacy. After a new game has ended, the button to view the game logs will show the logs for the latest game instead. The game log feature can be turned off by Server Administrators at any time for their Discord Server.

Mafia Remastered also stores your Authorization token when you authorize with the bot through the /connect slash command. Alongside with this, your refresh token is stored as well to ensure that the bot is able to generate a new authorization token using the refresh token. The only way we collect this is by you running the command mentioned above, and clicking on the button, and authorizing with the bot.

For users that wish to subscribe to the premium version of Mafia Remastered, we do not collect anything, besides your Discord User ID. However, your email and payment information are sent to Stripe. For more information on how Stripe collects your data, you may visit https://stripe.com/en-my/legal/privacy-center. We only collect your email, only IF you make a payment to Stripe, to subscribe to Mafia Remastered's premium plan.


Use of Data

Your Discord user ID is stored to distinguish you and other players when displaying your statistics. These statistics includes achievements, current balance (fake currency), win statistics and role statistics. Your statistics can be viewed by other people using Mafia Remastered.

Your info sent through modals and commands are used for game purposes. Your last will is shown when you die in the game, and your custom mafia death message is shown when you die to the mafia in the game. Your last will customization is used to display a more vibrant embed once you die, acting like a template.

Your Discord Username is stored in game logs, if the feature is enabled by Server Administrators. Any buttons and select menus you clicked during the game is also stored to ensure that you are not away from keyboard (AFK), and to show in the game logs about your actions.

Your Authorization token and Refresh token is stored with us for the only purpose of putting you into group DMs. We only collect this information once you authorize with Mafia Remastered that can be achieved through the /connect slash command.

Your email and payment information collected by Stripe is there to ensure that the payment process is smooth. Your payment information stays hidden to us, so Mafia Remastered has no options to look at your payment information. We can only access the email you used for subscribing to our plan, as a way to verify details should you request additional support. We do not reveal these email unless we can specifically confirm that the email in discussion is yours.


Data Retention and Deletion

As stated above, we collect your Discord user ID, your Discord username, the prompts you enter when using Mafia Remastered and the buttons or select menus you click. Some of your data will be deleted after a certain amount of time, depending on the use case and the activity of the Discord server.

Your Discord user ID is retained as long as you have used the bot once, and share at least 1 common Discord server with Mafia Remastered. If you leave all servers and don't share any common Discord servers, all of your data will be removed from us after one (1) week of you leaving from the last common Discord server shared between you.

Your Discord username and your actions (clicking a button or using the select menu) is retained until the game logs is refreshed, or after five (5) days. Alternatively, if the Discord server decides to disable the game log feature, your username will no longer be stored with us.

The items you buy from the shop that uses Mafia Remastered's (fake) economy, is tied with your Discord user ID, which is retained until your we no longer store you Discord user ID. This is also the case for game punishments and your authorization token as well as your refresh token.

Your authorization token and refresh token is given to us by Discord and is only valid until you de-authorize it. You may do so by going to your Discord profile, go to settings and disconnect from Mafia Remastered from Authorized Apps. This can be done through the Discord web client, the Discord mobile app or the desktop app.

You can request to delete all your data from Mafia Remastered. By doing so, any data you previously had will be completely wiped, with no backup or traces from us. After requesting, the data will be wiped after 7 days. Should you wish to cancel your action, you may do it within the 7 days, before your data gets wiped. To request for data deletion, you may join Mafia Remastered's official Discord support server and request for support.

Regarding saved payments, to delete your details, please visit the Stripe website, as your payment information is not stored with us. However, you may still request to delete your subscription history with us. Doing this will make you ineligible to request any refunds or support regarding certain payments.



Data Security

Mafia Remastered acknowledges that data security is very important and uses best practices to ensure that your sensitive data is not shared to anyone else. The only data we store that might be considered sensitive is your Discord user ID. Only the developers of Mafia Remastered have full access to your data. Mafia Remastered does not collect any other personal or sensitive data.

The data you provide through the modals and commands is not considered sensitive, as they are meant to be shown to other players in the game. Discord themselves also warn you that you should not enter sensitive information in the modals. We will not be held responsible if you decide to reveal your information to us, and that information is displayed to everyone else.

All payment information that you enter is not handled by us, but by Stripe. As mentioned above, you may view Stripe's privacy policy to see how they secure your data.



Changes to Privacy Policy

Users can use the /patch slash command to see info for any bot updates, privacy policy changes or terms of service changes.
